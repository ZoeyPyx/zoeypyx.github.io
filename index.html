Research & academic work

Existential Cognition: A Computational Analysis of Existential Discourse in Online Communities

Master's Honors ThesisUniversity of Chicago

A large-scale computational study of over 330,000 Reddit comments touching on existential thought, depression, anxiety, and emotional coping.

  • Applied topic modeling, semantic clustering, and transformer-based language models to surface psychological themes in online discourse.
  • Identified recurring domains: meaning and purpose, mortality awareness, uncertainty, and self-reflection.
  • Combined computational methods with psychological theory to study how people express existential concerns in naturalistic settings.
